About Us

Criminal is an award-winning podcast about true crime. Stories of people who’ve done wrong, been wronged, or gotten caught somewhere in the middle.

Criminal is one of the most popular podcasts in the world, and was named one of the Best Podcasts of 2023 by The New York Times. The show has been featured in “Best of” lists in Time, The Atlantic, Cosmopolitan, Rolling Stone, USA Today, Wired, and more. We travel around the country to interview people, and sometimes take the show on the road to tell stories live.
